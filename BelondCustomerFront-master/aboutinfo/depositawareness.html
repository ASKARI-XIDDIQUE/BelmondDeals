Deposit Awareness

Deposit can be done at the “Booking” Page and “Wallet” page. Click on the "Deposit" button to redirect to the online deposit customer service to assist in remittance Deposit. User’s can choose WhatsApp and Telegram to connect with customer care for more information and all financial related queries. After transferring the money according to the account provided by the customer service of the platform, make sure to submit the receipt or other relevant screenshots of the successful transfer to the account for the system verification purpose and Make sure the Beneficiary details of the person you're transferring to and the amount you're transferring are the same as what you're transferring. If you encounter any unsolvable problems during the Deposit process, please contact the on-duty online customer service. Due to a large amount of information, please make sure to check the Deposit account number of this platform carefully before making Deposit. The platform occasionally changes the Deposit account number. If you have any inquiries, please contact the platform online customer service.
